# Accounting

#### Accounting Defination
> __会计（Accounting）__ 可以被定义为一个 __记录__、__汇总__ 并 __分析__ 企业经济活动的系统性框架

大部分公司要记三套账
- __Three__ sets of books
  - __Financial accounting__
  > Standardized report for external stakeholders
  - __Tax accounting__
  > IRS rules for computing taxes payable
  - __Managerial accounting__
  > Custom reports for internal decision making

__What are the required Financial Reporting(财务报表)__
1. __Balance Sheet(资产负债表)__
    > Financial position on a specific date
2. __Income Statement(利润表)__
    > Results of operations over a period of time using accrual accounting
3. __Statement of Cash Flows(现金流量表)__
    > Sources and uses of cash over a period of time
4. __Statement of Stockholders' Equity(所有者权益变动表)__
    > Changes in stockholders' equity over a period of time
